## What This Means for Startups

Each pairing contrasts the **long-term compounding choice** against the **short-term optimization choice.** The left side builds durable capital; the right side extracts.

**Systems > Goals:** Build a research process, not a goal to find one idea. This vault is the system. Goals ("find a startup idea by April") produce anxiety and bad shortcuts.

**Principles > Tactics:** Understand why things work (PG's essays, Karpathy's thesis) rather than copying tactics. Tactics expire; principles generalize.

**Problems > Solutions:** Start from validated pain, not from a solution looking for a problem. Consistent with PG's "organic ideas" doctrine.

**People > Projects:** The network and relationships you build outlast any single project. SafeBiz KZ and VibeScan are projects. The founder-market knowledge you're building is the durable thing.

**Trends > News:** Track structural waves (AI coding shift, CIS market maturation) not daily noise.

**Writing > Reading:** External synthesis forces clarity. This vault exemplifies the principle.
